Game Aesthetics Review Heroes of Might and Magic V



It is a Role Playing Game combined with Turn-based strategic movements and battles. It has two different modes as Map-mode and War-mode. The ultimate goals are finding some valuable items, fighting with the enemies and conquer the factions' cities on the map in the end.

We will examine the game by these design sections of the game:
  • Menu Design
  • Map-mode Design
  • War-mode Design
  • Character / Artifact Design
  • City / Building Design
  • Cover Design

Menu Design

  • Tone / Light:
    • Reflective Control (Bright Look), Coincidence of Tone
  • Color:
    • Split Complementary Hue (Red, Yellow, Blue), Middle Saturated, Warm
  • Contrast / Affinity:
    • Contrast of Saturation
  • Space / Screen Division:
    • Golden Ratio (Menu Buttons to Background)
  • Placement:
    • Menu Buttons at bottom rightmost corner
  • Font:
    • Coherent with the Theme

Map-Mode Design

  • Tone / Light:
    • Reflective Control (Bright Look), Coincidence of Tone
  • Color:
    • Green Based, No Color Scheme -palette-
  • Contrast / Affinity:
    • Contrast of Hue
  • Space / Screen Division:
    • Top-down view, Aerial Diffusion, In-game menu buttons has a good ratio and position
  • Narration:
    • Player can choose who he fights -excluding main story-

War-Mode Design

  • Tone / Light:
    • Reflective Control, Coincidence of Tone
  • Color:
    • Green Based, Cool
  • Contrast / Affinity:
    • Contrast of Hue
  • Space / Screen Division:
    • 1:4 Horizontal Ratio (1 for Menu, 4 for Game Screen)
  • Movement:
    • Grids for the Combat Area
  • Unity:
    • Grids created it between creatures.
  • Emphasis:
    • On the combat area

Character / Artifact Design

  • Tone / Light:
    • Changes according to the character or artifact.
  • Color:
    • Coherent With Their Cities' and Factions' Designs
  • Unity:
    • Coherent With Their Cities' and Factions' Designs
  • Figure / Ground:
    • Good

City / Building Design

  • Tone / Light:
    • Reflective Bright look is at hand this city and buildings designs also.
  • Color:
    • Coherent With Their Cities' and Factions' Designs
  • Contrast / Affinity:
    • There is a contrast of saturation at the city design.
  • Space / Screen Division:
    • There is an aerial (the fog) and a textural diffusion at the city design.
  • Unity:
    • The buildings are united with the city. There are probably some statues over the buildings according to the relevance of this building.
  • Emphasis:
    • It is on the building selected when we enter into the city.

Cover Design

  • Tone / Light:
    • We can see the cover inspired with the incident control. There is a bright light at the back of the character; the character's and the city's color tones are determined by this lights. Also the character is in coincidence of tone.
  • Color:
    • There are three main colors in the cover: Yellow, Blue and Green. It is an analogic color scheme because this three colors are neinghbors on the color wheel.
  • Space / Screen Division:
    • There is a 1:1 Vertical Ratio between the main character and background of the cover.
  • Placement
    • There is golden ratio between the character's head including torso and the whole cover.
  • Movement:
    • There is a latin cross figure on the character's helmet, so it attracts attention to that area. Also the city's towers are very sharp and point to the name of the game.
  • Font:
    • The name of the game is written with a metalic grey and yellow font. Its number is written in Roman numeral type.
  • Emphasis:
    • It is on the main character on the cover's right hand side.
  • Unity:
    • The character on the cover looks like a holy knight from a fantasy world, and in the background there is a flying beast on the sky and the city is not structured as today's buildings, so there is very good bond and unity between the character and the whole cover's story.
  • Mood:
    • The character is looking to the camera, hence he has a demand mood.
  • Figure / Ground:
    • The character distinguishly seperated from the background with perspective, diffusion and focus differences.
  • Distance:
    • We are in the personal distance of the character on the cover.
  • Framing:
    • It is between medium and medium full shot.
  • Camera Angle:
    • It is a subjective camera angle and the character looks to the camera from straight on angle.
  • Symbolic Meanings:
    • The character is between the center and margin.
