M. Çağkan Uludağlı, PhD

Assistant Professor

Department of Computer Engineering, İzmir University of Economics

He received his BSc degree in Computer Engineering from İzmir Institute of Technology at 2012, his MSc degree in Game Technologies from Middle East Technical University at 2017, and his PhD degree in Computer Engineering from İzmir University of Economics at 2023. He worked as a software developer at METU Computer Center between 2013-2017, and as a research assistant at Computer Engineering department of our university between 2018-2024. He's been working as an Assistant Professor at the same department since November 2024.

His research interests cover the topics such as player-computer interaction, artificial intelligence for games, psychology in game design, virtual/augmented/mixed reality technologies and graph generators for social networks.
