Game Development The Flying Postman



It is a platform runner game which is made for our Game Development Pipeline class by a two person team. Your goal is to reach the house without sticking to the obstacles. You are a Postman who have magnetic shoes and you can walk on both ceiling and floor. But the shoes have limited powers to run on the ceiling. You don't want to fall between enemies. You can go between the ceiling and the floor with Space key.

In this webpage, I will examine aesthetical differences between the old version of our game and the improved one. I have improved the game according to the design principles I have learned in the class and with this knowledge, I have developed the game properly.

We will compare the differences in a sequence as the old one and the new one.
